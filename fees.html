Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about fees and registration

What is the registration fee and is it refundable?

The registration fee is P300 and is a one-time, non-refundable fee. It covers administrative costs, student file creation, and assessment placement.

Do you offer discounts for siblings?

Yes! We offer a 10% discount on monthly fees for the second child and 15% for the third child when siblings enroll together.

Can I pay fees in installments?

Yes, monthly payments are our standard arrangement. Fees are due at the beginning of each month. We also offer termly payment options for parents who prefer to pay in advance.

What happens if I miss a payment?

We understand that financial challenges can occur. Please contact our accounts department as soon as possible to arrange a payment plan. Extended non-payment may result in suspension of classes.

How do I apply for a scholarship?

To apply for a scholarship, please contact our admissions office at +267 123 4567 or email scholarships@emperitus.ac.bw. You will need to provide proof of financial need and academic records.

Is there a registration deadline?

We accept registrations throughout the year. However, spaces are limited, especially for our rewrite programmes. Early registration is recommended to secure a spot.
