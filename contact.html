Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my child?

You can register by visiting any of our branches, calling us, or filling out the contact form above. We'll guide you through the simple 5-step registration process.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute consultation to assess your child's needs and recommend the right programme. Contact us to schedule your appointment.

What is your response time?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours and answer calls during office hours. WhatsApp messages are answered within a few hours.
