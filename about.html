Our Story

Empowering Botswana's Students Since 2020

Emperitus Academy was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to give Botswana students a second chance at academic success. What started as a small group of passionate educators has grown into a trusted learning institution serving hundreds of students across the country.

Our journey began when our founder recognized that traditional classroom settings often fail to address individual learning needs. We believed that every student has unique potential waiting to be unlocked, and personalized attention is the key to academic success.

Today, Emperitus Academy has helped over 500 students improve their grades, build confidence, and develop lifelong learning skills. Our team of 30+ expert tutors continues to make a difference in students' lives every single day.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, personalized tutoring services that empower students to achieve academic excellence, build confidence, and develop critical thinking skills for lifelong success. We believe every student deserves a second chance to excel.

Our Vision

To become the leading tutoring institution in Botswana, recognized for transforming education through innovative teaching methods, dedicated educator-student partnerships, and a commitment to "Ever Learning, Ever Growing."

Scholarship Programme

At Emperitus Academy, we believe that financial circumstances should never be a barrier to quality education. Our scholarship programme supports underprivileged students who demonstrate academic potential and a strong desire to learn.

Scholarship benefits include:

  • Full or partial tuition fee coverage
  • Free study materials and resources
  • Mentorship and career guidance
