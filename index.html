About Emperitus Academy

Emperitus Academy was founded to give Botswana students a second chance at academic success. We specialize in helping students who have underperformed in national examinations, as well as primary, junior, and senior learners requiring extra academic support.

Our motto, "Ever Learning, Ever Growing," reflects our commitment to continuous improvement and lifelong education.
